ABOUT US

N'EREDITÀ is short for the Italian phrase “Nostra Eredita” which translates to “Our Legacy.” The name of the company comes from our desire to leave an impact and send a message to the youth.

Essentially, we want to pass down our legacy to the Bosconian youth, inspire them and give them a model or group to look up to, especially in the design of the products. Additionally, the use of Italian was brought up by the fact that Don Bosco’s mother tongue is Italian.
