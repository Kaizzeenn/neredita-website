LOGO & SLOGAN

What will your verse be?

A visual representation of the company's identity. It should be simple, memorable, and relevant to the company's product or service.

For N'EREDITÀ, it symbolizes the mission of letting Bosconians think about the lasting impact of decisions and actions, just as what St. John Bosco had set an example for. His influence still echoes among the students to this day, and that itself speaks volumes. The real legacy is not in material things one leaves behind but in values, lessons, and inspiration instilled into other people. With this slogan, N'EREDITÀ encourages the Bosconians to be proud of their individuality and their purpose in life, enabling them to create a mark that inspires and helps others.
